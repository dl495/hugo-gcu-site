Church Breakfasts

At the start of term, we put on a free breakfast on the first few Sunday mornings before heading off to church. There are lots of great churches in Cambridge, so we hope that coming to breakfast and hearing a bit about each church should make it much easier to find a church you like! We'll be meeting from 9am on 4th and 11th October in Old Kitchens (follow the signs!) and we'd love to see you there.

If you have any questions about churches in Cambridge, or just want to know a bit more, then let us know.
