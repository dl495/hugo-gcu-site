Getaway

We’ll be meeting together as a college group before the start of first term. This is a great chance to meet some Christians in college before Freshers’ Week starts, plus it will be great fun! Freshers are especially welcome! If you’d like to know more, just send us an email and we’ll tell you everything you need to know. Whilst you wait for our reply, you can find some information here.

Church Breakfasts

At the start of term, we put on a free breakfast on Sunday mornings before heading off to church. There are lots of great churches in Cambridge, so we hope that coming to breakfast and hearing a bit about each church should make it much easier to find a church you like! We'll be meeting from 9am on 4th and 11th October in Old Kitchens (follow the signs!) and we'd love to see you there.

If you have any questions about churches in Cambridge, or just want to know a bit more, then let us know.
