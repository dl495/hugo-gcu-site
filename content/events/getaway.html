Pre-Term Team Day


On Monday 28th September, just before International Freshers’ Week, the CUs of Sidney Sussex, Corpus Christi and St. Catharine’s Colleges will be joining GCU for a day of summer catch-up, gospel teaching, worship, games and much cake in the Stanley Library at Girton College (so maybe “Getaway” is pushing it a bit...).

We'd love this to be a day where as much of our community can come together to prepare to open our arms to all the new faces who will be coming to our college in the following week. So please sign up using the form below as soon as you can. If you need help with arranging early arrival, give us a shout.
